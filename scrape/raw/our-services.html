Services

Psychiatric Consultation

Connect with our partner psychiatrist, to avail comprehensive assessment of your mental health and receive medical & psychological interventions.

Psychotherapy

Our team of experienced clinical psychologists provide you with personalized treatment plans to help overcome mental health challenges and rediscover yourself.

Psychometric Assessments

We use standardized Psychometric tools for for emotional and behavioural assessment to aid in evaluation, design tailored intervention and ensure better prognosis.

School Mental Health Program

Mental wellbeing starts from young age. Through our programs specifically designed for schools, early identification of problems and imparting life skills for mental well-being can be achieved.

Corporate Mental Health Program

In corporate world, where people spend so much time working, mental health must be valued just as physical health for overall well-being. Awareness, screening and stress management workshops all combined in an interactive program. ​ ​

Public Awareness Programs

At Saday we aim to provide education and information regarding specific topics and current societal concerns to provoke action, community level understanding, mindful conversation about mental health conditions. ​ ​
