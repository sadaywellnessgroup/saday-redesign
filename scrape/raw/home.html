Why?

One in every 10 individuals in India suffers from a mental illness, many more suffer from psychological problems. Yet only 15% of sufferers ever see a mental health professional. Many of these problems can be solved and managed if awareness and skills for self-care are imparted to the community. 

'Saday wellness' comes out with this aim to provide wide range of mental health services. 

Learn More

What sets us apart?

A team of enthusiastic and compassionate mental health professionals with a wide forte of backgrounds. Psychiatrists, clinical psychologists and allied professionals dealing with mental health issues come together to bring comprehensive awareness programs, school and corporate wellness programs along with support groups for patients.
