Co-Founders

Dr. Aditya Agrawal, 

Psychiatrist

Social Media

Dr. Aditya is an enthusiastic psychiatrist who has experience in dealing with wide range of mental illness, addictions, childhood disorders and sexual problems. He has been a speaker for sessions conducted by Y20 summit and UNICEF India. His interest is in childhood and adolescent  mental health problems and addictions. Social Media

Saday Wellness Team

Dr. Kritika Chawla, 

Psychiatrist

Social Media

A young Doctor with a rich educational background, including an MBBS and MD in Psychiatry from KGMU Lucknow, Dr Kritika holds a wealth of experience garnered from esteemed institutions. Her approach to mental well-being is individualized to each patient, offering tailored therapy and medications to ensure effective treatment outcomes. Her passion extends beyond clinical practice; she has shared her expertise as a speaker at both state and national level conferences. Her engaging and empathetic communication skills make her the go-to professional to learn mindfulness techniques. 

Dr. Shivangini Singh, 

Psychiatrist

Social Media

Dr. Shivangini Singh, MD is a budding young Psychiatrist- Mental Health Expert, with experience of working in premium institutes like KGMU, Lucknow. She specialises in holistic mental well being with special focus on youth mental health and advocates better mental health by spreading awareness through various social media channels like YouTube and Instagram. Her expertise extends to anxiety disorders, deaddiction, child and adolescent mental health, and brain stimulation techniques in psychiatry.

Ms. Haifa Ainbosi, 

Psychologist and support group facilitator

Social Media

Ms Haifa is a budding psychologist who excels in organizing and facilitating workshops. She has managed several support groups on 'grief understanding' under Wildflower mental health. She believes in creating spaces for participants to freely express themselves. By using her unique background of design and arts, she creates engaging visuals to help combat mental health stigma. She has also conducted impactful workshops in rural areas on menstrual hygiene. Ms. Ainbosi often uses poetry as a means to foster body positivity and manage stress.

Clinical Psychologist

Social Media
