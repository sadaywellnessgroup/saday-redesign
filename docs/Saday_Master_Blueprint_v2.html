1. Executive Summary

Saday is a clinical-first mental health platform. Unlike generic booking sites, Saday focuses on the "Invisibles" in care: the family members (Informants), the longitudinal outcome data, and the strict routing of patients into five distinct care tracks.

2. The Informant Moat (L1/L2/L3 System)

This is the core differentiator. We enable family involvement without breaking patient confidentiality.

L1: Observer (Passive)

Caregiver submits weekly observation forms (mood, sleep, compliance). View: Only their own submissions. Benefit: Tracks "Insight-free" symptoms (Mania/Psychosis).

L2: Collateral (Active)

Informant invited for a specific session. View: Joint video room only. Benefit: Essential for ADHD/Dementia/Paediatric diagnostics.

L3: Guardian (Full)

Parent/Legal Guardian controls the account. Requirement: Mandatory for patients <12 years old.

3. The Saday Material Vault

A centralized storage system for clinical assets (PDFs, Videos, Homework). No more ad-hoc WhatsApp attachments.

  • Storage: Supabase S3 bucket (Mumbai).
  • Permission Engine: MHP "dispatches" a file; patient receives a WhatsApp link (MSG91) + a notification in their Patient Portal.
  • Tracking: System logs view_event. MHP can see if the patient actually read the "Anxiety Toolkit" before the next session.
