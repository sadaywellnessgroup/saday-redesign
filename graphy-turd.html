Integrative Somatic Trauma Therapy

A comprehensive framework for understanding the physiological effects of trauma. Learn evidence-based somatic interventions to support client recovery safely and effectively over this intensive 10-day program.

What You Will Learn

Physiology of Trauma

Understand nervous system dysregulation, survival responses, and polyvagal theory.

Somatic Tracking

Learn techniques to help clients safely track and discharge trapped survival energy.

Resourcing Skills

Build client capacity and resilience before attempting trauma reprocessing.

Clinical Integration

Seamlessly blend somatic approaches into your existing therapeutic modality.

PH.D., SEP, LPC
