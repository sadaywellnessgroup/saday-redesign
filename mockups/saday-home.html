Why Saday

One in ten Indians lives with mental illness. Only 15% ever see a professional.

Many of these struggles can be managed — when awareness and the skills for self-care reach the people who need them. Saday exists to close that gap, with a wide range of accessible mental-health services for individuals, schools, and workplaces.

What we offer

Complete mental-health solutions

From a single consultation to long-term care — psychiatrists, clinical psychologists, and allied professionals, together under one roof.

Psychiatric consultation

Comprehensive assessment with our psychiatrists, plus medical and psychological interventions tailored to you.

Psychotherapy

Personalised treatment plans from experienced clinical psychologists to help you overcome challenges and rediscover yourself.

Psychometric assessments

Standardised tools for emotional and behavioural assessment — to evaluate, tailor intervention, and improve outcomes.

School mental-health

Programs designed for schools — early identification of problems and life-skills for well-being from a young age.

Corporate well-being

Awareness, screening, and stress-management workshops — combined in one interactive program for teams.

Support groups

Facilitated peer spaces where you realise you're not alone — open, safe, and led by trained professionals.
